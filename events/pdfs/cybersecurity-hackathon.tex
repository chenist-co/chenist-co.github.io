\begin{tcolorbox}[colback=primary!10, colframe=primary, title=\textbf{Event Overview}]
The Cybersecurity Hackathon is a 48-hour challenge where participants work in teams to solve real-world cybersecurity problems. Open to security enthusiasts of all levels, from beginners to experts, this event provides a platform to develop and showcase your skills while networking with industry professionals.

\textit{Note: Details, dates, and themes are subject to change.}
\end{tcolorbox}

\sectiontitle{Challenge Format}

Teams of 2-4 participants will tackle a series of increasingly difficult cybersecurity challenges across multiple domains. The event combines elements of Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions with practical security implementation scenarios that mirror real-world situations.

\sectiontitle{Challenge Categories}

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
    \item \textbf{Web Application Security}: Identify and exploit vulnerabilities in web applications
    \item \textbf{Network Security}: Analyze and secure network infrastructure
    \item \textbf{Cryptography}: Break and implement cryptographic systems
    \item \textbf{Reverse Engineering}: Analyze compiled applications to understand their functionality
    \item \textbf{Forensics}: Investigate digital evidence to reconstruct security incidents
    \item \textbf{Social Engineering}: Develop strategies to counter social engineering attacks
\end{itemize}

\sectiontitle{Frequently Asked Questions}

\textbf{Do I need to be a cybersecurity expert to participate?}\\
No, we welcome participants of all skill levels. Teams are encouraged to include members with diverse experience levels, and mentors will be available to assist throughout the event.

\textbf{What should I bring?}\\
Participants should bring their own laptops, chargers, and any preferred peripherals. We'll provide power, high-speed internet, and a comfortable workspace.

\textbf{Is there a participation fee?}\\
Details soon. All meals and materials during the event are covered by our sponsors.

\textbf{Will accommodation be provided?}\\
Accommodation is not included, but we have arranged special rates with nearby hotels. Details will be provided to registered participants.

\textbf{Can I participate remotely?}\\
This hackathon is designed as an in-person event to maximize collaboration and mentorship opportunities. Remote participation is not available.
