About Clubul Computer

Clubul Computer is a community-driven technology club dedicated to promoting digital literacy, computer science education, and technological innovation for all ages. Through regular meetups, workshops, and collaborative projects, we create an environment where members can learn, experiment, and grow their technical skills while building meaningful connections.

Our Mission

To make technology accessible, understandable, and enjoyable for everyone, regardless of age or background, by creating inclusive learning environments and supportive communities.

Core Values:

  • Inclusivity: Creating a welcoming space for people of all backgrounds
  • Collaboration: Encouraging knowledge sharing and teamwork
  • Hands-on Learning: Emphasizing practical experience and experimentation
  • Community Impact: Using technology to address local challenges

What We Offer

  • Regular Meetups: Monthly gatherings to discuss technology trends, share projects, and solve problems together
  • Workshops: Hands-on learning sessions for various technologies and skills
  • Youth Programs: Age-appropriate tech education for children and teens
  • Mentorship: Connecting beginners with experienced practitioners
  • Project Collaboration: Opportunities to work on real-world projects with others
  • Resources Library: Access to learning materials, tools, and equipment

Membership & Participation

Clubul Computer welcomes participants of all backgrounds and skill levels, from complete beginners to experienced professionals.

How to Join

Becoming a part of Clubul Computer is simple and open to all interested in technology:

  • Attend any of our open events or monthly meetups
  • Register on our community platform for updates and discussions
  • Volunteer to help organize events or lead workshops
  • Propose a project or initiative aligned with our mission

There are no formal membership requirements or fees, though some specialized workshops/trainings may have material costs.
