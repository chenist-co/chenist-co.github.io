# Chen.ist Academy Announces Summer School 2025 Program

**CRAIOVA, ROMANIA** â€” Chen.ist Academy is pleased to announce its Summer School 2025, an intensive month-long program focusing on cybersecurity, cloud computing, and artificial intelligence. The program will be held from July 20 to August 20, 2025, at a private location in Craiova, Romania.

## Program Overview

The Summer School 2025 is designed to provide participants with comprehensive introductory-level education in specialized technical domains. The program combines theoretical lectures, hands-on workshops, group projects, and networking opportunities to create a rich learning environment.

## Curriculum Highlights

The curriculum includes:

- **Core Introductory Courses:** Cybersecurity Fundamentals, Network Security Basics, Secure Coding Principles
- **Specialized Introductory Trainings:** Ethical Hacking Fundamentals, Incident Response Basics, Security Architecture Principles
- **Microlearning Modules:** Including Security Tool Spotlights, Command Line Essentials, Git Fundamentals, and more

## Who Should Attend

The program is targeted toward:

- Students pursuing degrees in computer science or related fields
- Early-career professionals looking to expand their skill set
- Career changers transitioning into tech roles
- Educators seeking to update their technical knowledge

## Registration Information

Registration opens on July 7, 2025. Early registration is recommended as spaces are limited to 50 participants per track to ensure a high-quality, personalized experience.

## Post-Program Opportunities

After completing the Summer School, participants are encouraged to apply their new skills at upcoming events, including:

- SpaceApps Challenge 2025 (October 3-5, 2025)
- Cybersecurity Hackathon (November 15-17, 2025)

## About Chen.ist Academy

Chen.ist Academy is dedicated to providing high-quality technology education and training. Our programs are designed to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry requirements, preparing participants for successful careers in the rapidly evolving tech landscape.
