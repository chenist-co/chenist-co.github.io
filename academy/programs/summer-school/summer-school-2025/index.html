Summer School 2025

An intensive month-long program focusing on cybersecurity, cloud computing, and artificial intelligence, with specialized tracks for each area.

July 20 - August 20, 2025 Tech Campus, Bucharest Registration opens July 7

Event Overview

The Summer School 2025 is an intensive month-long program focused on cutting-edge technologies and practical skills development. Perfect for students and professionals looking to enhance their technical capabilities in a condensed, immersive format.

Over the course of the program, participants will:

  • Engage with industry experts through daily workshops and lectures
  • Work on real-world projects in small teams
  • Build a comprehensive portfolio of technical work
  • Network with peers and potential employers
  • Receive personalized mentorship and feedback

Program Details

The Summer School 2025 program is organized into three specialized tracks:

Cybersecurity Track

Develop practical skills in protecting digital assets, identifying vulnerabilities, and implementing security solutions.

  • Threat analysis and detection
  • Network security implementation
  • Security policies and procedures
  • Ethical hacking principles
Four weeks, July 20 - August 20

Cloud Computing Track

Master modern cloud platforms, architecture, and deployment strategies for scalable applications.

  • Cloud infrastructure design
  • Container orchestration
  • Serverless architecture
  • DevOps practices
Four weeks, July 20 - August 20

AI & ML Track

Explore artificial intelligence and machine learning fundamentals with hands-on project work.

  • Machine learning fundamentals
  • Neural networks and deep learning
  • Natural language processing
  • Computer vision applications
Four weeks, July 20 - August 20

Schedule

The Summer School runs for four weeks, with a balanced mix of lectures, workshops, and project work:

Week 1: Fundamentals & Introduction

Day Morning Afternoon
Monday Opening Ceremony & Introduction Technology Landscape Overview
Tuesday Track-Specific Fundamentals Hands-on Lab Sessions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cost to attend?

The full program fee is €1,500, which includes all materials, lunches, and special events. Early bird registrants receive a 15% discount. Limited scholarships are available for students.

Is accommodation provided?

Accommodation is not included in the program fee. However, we can assist with recommendations and have partnered with nearby hotels to offer discounted rates.

What level of technical knowledge is required?

Participants should have basic programming experience (in any language) and familiarity with web concepts. A pre-course assessment and learning resources will be provided to help you prepare.

Will I receive a certificate?

Yes, all participants who complete the program will receive a certificate of completion. Additionally, top performers will receive special recognition.

Can I attend virtually?

The program is designed as an in-person experience to maximize hands-on learning and networking. However, select lectures may be available for remote viewing.
