Welcome to our Academy

The chen.ist Academy is a comprehensive educational initiative dedicated to advancing technical knowledge, fostering innovation, and building a community of skilled professionals. Through our diverse range of courses, events, and projects, we aim to provide practical, hands-on learning experiences that prepare participants for the challenges of modern technology environments.

Learning Paths

Our academy offers structured learning paths designed to guide your educational journey based on your interests, goals, and current skill level.

Cybersecurity Track

A comprehensive learning path for those interested in building careers in cybersecurity, from foundational concepts to specialized areas.

  • Courses: Practical Cybersecurity, Human Firewall, Data Protection
  • Events: Cybersecurity Hackathon, Security Workshops
  • Projects: Vulnerability Assessment, Security Tool Development
View Path Details

Open Source Development Track

For developers looking to enhance their skills through contribution to open source projects and collaboration with the community.

  • Courses: Open Source Development, Unix/Linux Systems
  • Events: Computer Club Meetups, Coding Workshops
  • Projects: Open Source Contributions, Tool Development
View Path Details

Innovation & Maker Track

Designed for creators and innovators interested in hardware, IoT, and physical computing projects.

  • Courses: Hardware Fundamentals, IoT Security
  • Events: Makers Workshop, Space Hackathon
  • Projects: IoT Prototyping, Hardware Development
View Path Details

Data Privacy & Compliance Track

For professionals focused on data protection, privacy regulations, and implementing compliant systems.

  • Courses: Data Protection and Privacy, Security Awareness
